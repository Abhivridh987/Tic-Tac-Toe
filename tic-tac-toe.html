Tic - Tac - Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a simple two-player game played on a 3×3 grid. Players take turns marking a square with their symbol—X or O. The goal is to be the first to align three of your symbols in a row, column, or diagonal. It’s a quick, strategic game that encourages players to think ahead and block their opponent. Despite its simplicity, it’s a classic that never gets old!
